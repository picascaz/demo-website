Core engines 三大引擎

Our privacy and security engine 我们的隐私和安全引擎

Sentry, Cloak, and Asset Partition — one architecture for bank-grade USDT operations. 哨兵、斗篷与资产分区 — 一套面向银行级 USDT 运营的架构。

S

D4U Sentry engine D4U 哨兵引擎

Real-time KYT, 16-layer shielding, smart alerts. 实时 KYT、十六层防护、智能预警。

Real-time Monitoring 实时监控

Millisecond-level KYT detection, instant scanning upon receipt, zero-delay isolation of risky funds. 毫秒级 KYT 检测，入账即扫描，零延迟隔离风险资金。

16-Layer Protection 16 层防护

Covers on-chain tracing, mixer identification, tainted USDT databases, sanction lists, and 16 detection dimensions in total. 覆盖链上追溯、混币识别、问题 USDT 数据库、制裁名单等 16 个检测维度。

Intelligent Alerts 智能预警

Three-tier risk classification (high, medium, low), automatic isolation of suspicious transactions to the observation zone. 高中低风险三级分类，自动将可疑交易隔离至观察区。

C

D4U Cloak Mode — Zero-Trace Payment D4U 斗篷模式 — 零痕迹支付系统

Path obfuscation, metadata zeroing, rotating addresses. 路径混淆、元数据清零、地址轮换。

Path Obfuscation 路径混淆

Uses privacy routing to scatter transaction characteristics and sever address linkage. 通过 D4U 混合网络与隐私路由打散交易特征，切断地址关联。

Metadata Zeroing 元数据清零

Records no metadata — including transfer timing, amount patterns, or counterparties. 不记录转账时间、金额模式、交易对手等任何元数据。

Address Rotation 地址轮换

Generates a fresh temporary address per transfer; it is retired immediately after use. 每笔交易自动生成临时地址，用后即焚，不留痕迹。

Multi-signature and hardware wallet–grade protection. 多重签名 + 硬件钱包级防护。

Flow Zone 流动区

Instant receipt and fast turnover. 即时到账，快速周转。

Watch Zone 观察区

Foundation 基石

Built on a Foundation of Security and Privacy 建立在安全与隐私的基石之上

Self-Custody, Always 始终自托管

D4U is fully non-custodial — your keys, your coins. We never hold your funds. D4U 完全非托管 — 你的密钥，你的资产。我们从不持有你的资金。

Open Source by Default 默认开源

Core components are open source. You don't have to trust our word — you can verify the security yourself. 核心组件开源。你不必听信我们的承诺 — 可以亲自验证安全性。

Hybrid Mixnet 混合网络

Our hybrid mixnet breaks and reshuffles transaction paths, so your flows can't be easily traced back to you. 混合网络打散并重排交易路径，让你的资金流难以被轻易回溯到你。

Zero-Knowledge Built In 内置零知识证明

Native ZK proofs let you prove balances or compliance without revealing your actual holdings or transaction history. 原生 ZK 证明让你证明余额或合规，而无需暴露真实持仓或交易历史。
